How to Use This Simulator

1. Hover to the "Enter your primogems" section and enter the amount of primogems (in-game currency) that you currently have then hit the Enter Key on your keyboard.
2. Choose the banner that you want to summon for by using the arrows on the sides of the page
3. Click on the summon button to see your results!

Note: If you want to see your results after a certain pity keep on rolling until the desired pity number
appears on the result page then once you come back to the summon page hover to the "Enter your primogems" section again and input your current primogems. If you want to start the simulator over keeping rolling until the current pity hits 90 for the Character results page and 70 for the Weapons results page then you can start the simulator again from Step 1.
